Her Cake

Her Cake tells the story of a girl who runs a bakrey, intertwined with other stories. Whose stories are they?

The inspiration for the story comes from immersive videos that are popular on the internet,
which always focus on the object itself, reflflecting a desirable way of life behind it.
I love watching and cooking-related videos, and the sound of cream squeezing always makes me calm down.
Her Cake shows my fantasy for this dreamy life, although the reality is certainly not like this.
