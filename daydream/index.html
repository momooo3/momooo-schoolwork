Daydream

Daydreaming is an outlet for desires that can't be realized in reality.
People are always ashamed to show their daydreams to others,
because they are the most secret desires.

Have you ever been alone in your own world when the dream is interrupted by a sudden external stimulus, and for a moment shame, remorse, and emptiness fill your mind and body instead of pleasant fantasies? This series of photos hope to show a carefully arranged, vigorous, blurred, dazzling, fading illusion through flowers.
